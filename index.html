Must be nice Album

Released September 26, 2012

Other Hits:

  • I mean It
  • Me, Myself & I
  • No Limit

With his self-released first full-length album, “Must Be Nice,” he reached third place on the iTunes Hip-Hop Chart in September of that year.It’s a shame G-Eazy’s debut album didn’t get more love when it was released back in 2012, as it contains some of the Oakland native’s best tracks, with “Must Be Nice” serving as a prime example. At the time the song was a glimpse into the rapper’s future, a seamless blend of sincere lyrics and witty insights layered atop a beat that doesn’t take itself too seriously. Nowadays the only thing that’s changed is our expectations, but thankfully G-Eazy has been more than willing to keep pumping out the head bobbin’ hits.
